# **Organizational Overview of Mrizi i Zanave** #

**Mrizi i Zanave** is an agritourism enterprise located in the village of **Fishtë in northern Albania**, founded by the Albanian chef **Altin Prenga**. The business represents a modern model of rural tourism where agriculture, gastronomy, hospitality, and local production operate together. Originally started as a restaurant focused on traditional Albanian cuisine made with locally sourced ingredients, the enterprise has expanded into a full agritourism complex that includes a restaurant, guesthouse, farm, and food-processing facilities.

The company follows the farm-to-table philosophy, meaning that most ingredients used in the restaurant are produced directly on the farm or supplied by nearby rural families. This creates a closed production cycle where agricultural production, food processing, and gastronomy are interconnected. Visitors not only enjoy traditional food but can also experience rural tourism activities such as farm tours and food-tasting experiences.

Due to its diverse activities, the organization operates through multiple departments including restaurant operations, agriculture, hospitality, logistics, and administration. Managing the flow of information between these departments requires coordination and efficient data management, making the business a suitable case study for the development of an integrated software management system.

# **Software System Description**  #

The software will consist of several main functional modules:

## **1. Reservation Management Module** ##

This module will manage reservations for both the restaurant and the hotel. Staff will be able to register customer bookings by specifying the date, time, number of guests, and special requests. The system will display room and table availability in real time and prevent double bookings. It will also store the history of reservations for future reference.

## **2. Restaurant Management Module** ##

This module will support the daily operations of the restaurant. It will manage menu items, customer orders, and billing. The system will record orders placed by customers and automatically update the list of sold items. This helps staff track popular dishes and manage service more efficiently.

## **3. Product Sales Management Module** ##

Mrizi i Zanave also sells traditional products such as cheese, wine, jam, and other local specialties. This module will manage the sale of these products by recording product information, prices, and quantities available for sale. It will also track sales transactions and maintain a record of purchased products.


## **4. Inventory Management Module** ##

This module will monitor the stock of products and ingredients used in the restaurant or sold to customers. The system will automatically update the quantity of products after each sale and notify staff when stock levels are low.


## **5. Reporting and Analytics Module** ##

The system will generate reports that help management analyze business performance. Examples include:

restaurant sales reports
product sales reports
number of hotel reservations
revenue from each service
